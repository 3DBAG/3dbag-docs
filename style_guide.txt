Colors
-------

Colors are in RGBA

- black: 000000ff

- grey: 999999ff

- red highlight: ff0000ff

- blue light: b4dde6ff

- blue medium: 24a1c8ff

- blue dark: 2d5fc1ff

Fonts
------

- text body: Inter ( https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter?preview.text_type=custom#about )

- monospace: Roboto Mono ( https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Roboto+Mono?preview.text_type=custom#about )
